### Award Details

**Organization:** Television Academy

### Achievement Summary
Emmy Award recognizing the innovation and excellence of the TWCTV Application — comprehensive television delivery platform enabling Time Warner Cable to serve subscribers across set-top box, web, and mobile platforms during critical industry transition period.

### Achievement Context
TWCTV Application represented critical innovation during rapid market shift from traditional cable TV to OTT consumption:
- **Platform Modernization:** Unified application serving traditional and OTT platforms
- **DRM Security:** Implemented just-in-time encryption protecting content and ad revenue
- **Dynamic Monetization:** Server-side ad insertion enabling targeted advertising
- **Scale:** Serving millions of Time Warner Cable subscribers
- **Technical Excellence:** Broadcast-quality delivery across diverse device platforms
