# Story Index

*Reserved for narrative-style career storytelling and case studies.*

**Status:** Empty (by design) — available for future use when longer-form narratives or case study development is needed.

---

**Purpose:** This index is reserved for future narrative context that goes beyond structured role/project/skills data. Examples might include:

- Career transition narratives
- Deep case studies with learnings and insights
- Market trend analysis and positioning documents
- Thought leadership pieces
- Speaking topics and presentation frameworks
- Interview preparation narratives
- Career philosophy and decision-making approaches

**When to use:** Create story files here when developing custom narratives for specific opportunities or when crafting longer-form portfolio pieces beyond what's captured in the structured context files.

**Format:** Narrative-style markdown files (*.md) that tell career stories rather than list achievements.
