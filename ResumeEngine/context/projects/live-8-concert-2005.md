# Live 8 Concert
## 2005
## AOL

### Summary
Emmy Award-winning live streaming event directing content acquisition, transcoding, and distribution across eight global concert sites. Set world record for concurrent live streaming, delivering multi-site international concert event to millions of viewers simultaneously. Demonstrated feasibility of global-scale live streaming during era when broadcast live streaming was nascent technology.

### Business Context
Live 8 was a global benefit concert series held simultaneously across eight major cities worldwide (London, Paris, Rome, Philadelphia, Tokyo, Rio de Janeiro, Moscow, Cornwall). AOL secured exclusive streaming rights and needed to deliver high-quality live streams of all eight concert venues to internet viewers worldwide. This represented unprecedented challenge in live streaming scale and complexity for early 2000s technology.

### Technical Challenge
- **Multi-Site Ingest:** Coordinating content acquisition from eight geographically distributed concert locations
- **Global Distribution:** Delivering live streams to millions of concurrent viewers via internet (streaming technology was in infancy)
- **Quality Standards:** Maintaining broadcast-quality video and audio across multiple encoding and delivery hops
- **Latency Management:** Minimizing streaming latency to keep remote viewers synchronized with live event
- **Infrastructure Limitations:** Operating within constraints of early 2000s internet bandwidth and streaming technology

### Technical Approach
- **Multi-Site Architecture:** Coordinated content acquisition from eight concert venues
- **Broadcast Operations Center:** Leveraged newly built all-digital downlink center for stream coordination and encoding
- **Encoding & Transcoding:** Real-time video encoding and format transcoding for streaming delivery
- **Distribution:** Internet streaming distribution to millions of concurrent viewers
- **Redundancy:** Built redundancy into acquisition and encoding to prevent single points of failure

### Major Achievements
- **World Record:** Set world record for live streaming — most concurrent users streaming live video at that time
