# Live Actions — Interactive Olympic Viewing Experience

## Project Summary

Interactive tool enabling Peacock viewers to choose their own viewing journey during the Paris 2024 Olympics. Built from ground up by sole architect—enabling viewers to follow specific athletes or events in real-time with frame-accurate overlays. Featured as cornerstone of Emmy-winning interactive experience for Olympics coverage.

## Background & Situation

After early user testing of a new interactive feature, the core product/design team received overwhelmingly negative feedback. The original idea was scrapped, and the team was frozen—stuck in analysis paralysis, stressed, and unsure how to proceed. Risk of missing roadmap timelines.

## Key Contribution

**Sole Architect & Builder**: End-to-end product development from concept through production deployment.

### Key Actions

- Took 4AM train to NYC—scheduled in-person whiteboard workshop for next morning
- Prepped the space, whiteboards, sticky notes, and facilitation plan
- Led "no bad ideas" brainstorm to encourage open submission
- Facilitated rapid down-selection based on user impact, tech feasibility, business value
- Architected end-to-end system and coordinated engineering implementation
- Designed and deployed frame-accurate overlay system

## Key Results

- Team recovered and selected new direction within single morning session
- **Live Actions** launched during Olympics (e.g., "keep watching soccer?")
- Feature became **global standard across Peacock, Sky Showtime, and Showmax**
- Also used for contextual ad overlays and promo interactions (e.g., "Add Oppenheimer to My Stuff")
- Patent filed under NDA for overlay trigger logic: **US-20250203152-A1** (Timed Metadata for Overlays)
- Boosted team confidence and delivered measurable innovation win under pressure

## Scale & Impact

- Deployed across Peacock, Sky Showtime, Showmax globally
- Millions of fans used Live Actions to customize Olympic experience
- Part of Paris 2024 Olympics coverage that generated:
  - 23.5 billion streaming minutes
  - 82% viewership increase vs. Tokyo 2020
  - All-time streaming record for Peacock platform

## Technology Stack

- Real-time metadata synchronization
- Frame-accurate overlay rendering
- Interactive viewer journey selection
- Live and VOD overlay support
- Multi-platform deployment (iOS, tvOS, web)

## Patents Generated
