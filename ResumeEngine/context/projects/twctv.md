# TWCTV Application — Streaming Platform for Cable Customers

## Project Summary

Emmy Award-winning OTT streaming application serving millions of Time Warner Cable customers. Key principal architect for VOD and advertising integration. Pioneered DRM and dynamic ad insertion during OTT inflection point in cable industry.

## Role & Contribution

**Principal Architect (VOD & Advertising)**: Led architecture for TWCTV VOD platform and advertising integration. Key lead on team creating the TWCTV application.

## Key Focus Areas

- TWCTV application architecture for OTT streaming
- VOD content acquisition, processing, and distribution
- Dynamic ad insertion and monetization strategies
- DRM (just-in-time encryption) implementation
- Centralized headend architecture for multi-region VOD delivery

## Key Results

- **Emmy Award** (2014) for innovation in OTT streaming platform
- Application served millions of Time Warner Cable customers
- Recognized for design and delivery of competitive OTT platform during industry transition

## Technology Stack

- OTT/VOD architecture
- DRM (just-in-time encryption)
- Dynamic ad insertion
- CDN-based distribution
- Set Top Box integration
- Content acquisition and processing systems

## Scale & Impact

- Millions of cable customers
- Multi-region VOD deployment
- Demonstrated cable operator competitiveness with OTT platforms (Netflix, Hulu, etc.)
- Industry recognition through Emmy Award
