# AOL Broadcast Operations Center — Digital Infrastructure Innovation

## Project Summary

Design and build of AOL's first all-digital broadcast operations center (BOC) specifically for streaming and OTT content. Multi-million dollar facility featuring 100+ audio channels and 34+ video channels with redundancy and master control override. Became foundation for all major live event operations including Emmy Award-winning Live 8 Concert.

## Role & Contribution

**Lead Architect & Builder**: Identified need, secured multi-million-dollar budget, designed architecture, selected vendors, managed build-out, and provided operational launch support.

## Key Actions

- Identified that existing BOC was unstable, low-capacity, and under-equipped for projected growth
- **Secured multi-million-dollar budget** and executive approval for ground-up replacement
- Designed facility with 100+ audio channels and 34+ video channels
- Implemented redundancy, monitoring, and kids-focused streaming studio
- Managed full vendor lifecycle: finding, vetting, negotiating, architecting, managing
- Delivered runbooks, trained operations staff, provided day-one launch support

## Key Results

- **AOL's first fully digital downlink facility** for live streaming
- Successfully supported Live 8 concert streams — **Emmy Award winner**
- Supported 24/7 operations including war coverage, news events, kids-focused content
- Became foundation for large-scale live event operations across AOL properties
- Enabled concurrent streaming of multiple content types (video, audio, mixed)

## Technical Specifications

- 100+ audio channels
- 34+ video channels
- Master control override system
- Redundancy and failover systems
- Kids-focused streaming studio
- 24/7 operations support

## Scale & Impact
