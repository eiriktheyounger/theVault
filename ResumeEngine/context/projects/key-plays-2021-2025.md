# Key Plays
## 2021 – 2025
## NBCUniversal

### Summary
Emmy Award-winning AI/ML-powered near real-time highlights feature designed and delivered for NBCU streaming and broadcast platforms. Critical infrastructure for sports coverage across multiple leagues and international events, serving millions of viewers. Architected multi-platform interactive highlights product utilizing advanced AI/ML technologies to detect, process, and deliver compelling sports moments to audiences in near real-time.

### Business Context
NBCU's streaming and broadcast platforms needed to compete with social media and clip-sharing platforms by delivering personalized, real-time highlights to viewers. Key Plays anticipated growing audience demand for interactive and AI-personalized sports experiences, enabling viewers to quickly discover the most exciting moments from live broadcasts.

### Technical Approach
- **AI/ML Architecture:** Near real-time highlight detection using machine learning models trained on sports content
- **Multi-Platform Delivery:** Designed for delivery across NBCU Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) products, streaming and broadcast platforms
- **Scale:** Processing live feeds from multiple sports and leagues simultaneously
- **Infrastructure:** Cloud-based architecture supporting millions of concurrent users
