# TWC VOD Centralization — 350% Ad Revenue Growth

## Project Summary

Major VOD operations transformation from distributed manual processes to centralized automated workflows using SaaS platforms. Architected catch-and-encode pipelines and VMS/CMS system migrations enabling 350%+ boost in ad revenue. Directed vendor selection, negotiation, and integration across multiple hardware, software, and SaaS providers.

## Role & Contribution

**Architect & Program Lead**: Identified automation opportunities, designed centralized architecture, directed vendor selection, and managed SaaS transition.

## Key Actions

- Identified need for automated, scalable content operations
- **Architected catch-and-encode workflow** replacing manual processes
- **Transitioned legacy VMS/CMS to SaaS platforms**
- Found, vetted, and negotiated SaaS provider relationships
- Architected multi-vendor integration across hardware, software, and SaaS solutions
- Established centralized headend for all VOD/ad content
- Unified workflows across regions to improve scalability
- Directed website development for TWC homepage reducing editorial man-hours

## Key Results

- **350%+ boost in ad revenue** through automation and content optimization
- Significantly reduced editorial man-hours
- Eliminated manual VOD content processing
- Improved scalability across multi-region deployments
- SaaS platform relationship became long-term partnership
- Foundation for TWCTV application's content pipeline

## Technology Stack

- VMS/CMS (Video Management System / Content Management System)
- SaaS content management platforms
- Catch-and-encode workflow automation
- Advertisement operations systems
- Multi-region centralized headend
- Website development and CMS

## Scale & Impact

- Multi-region VOD deployment
- Millions of cable customers served
- 350%+ ad revenue increase
- Operational cost reduction through automation
- Scalability for OTT platform expansion

## Resume Bullet Language

- Architected catch-and-encode workflow transition that boosted ad revenue by over 350% and automated video operations across multiple regions
