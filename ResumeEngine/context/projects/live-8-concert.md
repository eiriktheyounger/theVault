# Live 8 Concert — World Record Live Streaming Event

## Project Summary

Emmy Award-winning global music concert streaming event from eight simultaneous sites worldwide. Set world record for concurrent live event streaming on the internet. Delivered live acquisition, transcoding, and distribution of high-profile music event to millions of simultaneous viewers.

## Role & Contribution

**Director of Acquisition, Transcoding & Distribution**: Led all technical operations from source acquisition through user playback.

## Key Accomplishments

### Broadcasting Operations Center (BOC) Support

Leveraged AOL's newly built Broadcast Operations Center to deliver global coverage from eight sites simultaneously. BOC infrastructure—featuring 100+ audio channels and 34+ video channels—provided the foundation for unprecedented concurrent streaming.

## Key Results

- **World record for concurrent live streaming** at the time
- **Emmy Award** (2005) for technical achievement in pioneering live streaming
- Successful delivery of high-profile music event to millions of viewers
- Validated live streaming as viable distribution channel for major broadcast content
- Set benchmark for future live event streaming architecture
