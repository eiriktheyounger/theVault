# Olympic Daily Recap
## 2021 – 2025
## NBCUniversal

### Summary
Generative AI-powered daily recap product featuring AI-synthesized narration in the voice of legendary broadcaster Al Michaels. Generated 7M+ personalized variants, delivering custom Olympics recap experiences to individual viewers. Pioneered GenAI application to sports broadcasting with production-quality voice synthesis and narrative personalization.

### Business Context
During Olympics coverage, NBCU needed to scale personalized content delivery to millions of viewers across geographies and time zones. Rather than producing thousands of individual recap videos manually, the Olympic Daily Recap applied Generative AI to create millions of personalized variants, each tailored to individual viewer interests and featuring Al Michaels' iconic voice narration.

### Technical Approach
- **Generative AI Pipeline:** Multi-stage GenAI system for recap narrative generation
- **Voice Synthesis:** GenAI voice synthesis replicating Al Michaels' speaking style and vocal characteristics
- **Personalization:** Dynamic recap generation tailored to individual viewer preferences and sports interests
- **Scale:** Generated 7M+ distinct personalized variants from single editorial input
- **Quality Control:** Maintained broadcast-quality narration and narrative coherence across all variants

### Major Achievements
- **7M+ Variants:** Generated over 7 million personalized recap variants from single editorial input
- **Voice Synthesis:** Successfully synthesized Al Michaels' voice across all variants using GenAI technologies
- **Viewer Engagement:** Delivered personalized recap experiences to Olympic viewers across time zones and geographies
- **Innovation:** Pioneered large-scale GenAI application to broadcast sports content
- **Production Quality:** Maintained editorial and voice quality standards while scaling to millions of variants

### Technologies & Tools
- **Generative AI:** LLM-based narrative generation, multi-stage content creation pipeline
- **Voice Synthesis:** AI voice synthesis for personality replication, vocal quality maintenance
- **Personalization:** User profile analysis, preference-based content generation
- **Video/Audio:** Real-time audio/video assembly, delivery system integration
- **Infrastructure:** Cloud-based GenAI pipeline, scalable personalization engine
- **Standards:** Broadcast audio standards, streaming delivery standards

### Impact & Outcomes

- Set new benchmark for AI-driven sports content delivery
- Enhanced viewer engagement during Olympics coverage period
