# AOL Broadcast Operations Center (BOC)
## 2001 – 2006
## AOL

### Summary
First all-digital broadcast operations center designed specifically for streaming and OTT content. Pioneering facility managing 100+ radio channels and 34+ video channels with dedicated streaming studio, supporting 24/7 live and on-demand content delivery. Designed and built to support Live 8 Concert production and AOL's expanding streaming content portfolio.

### Business Context
AOL's existing broadcast operations center was designed for traditional broadcast and satellite distribution, with streaming as an afterthought. As AOL identified rapid growth in online video consumption, the company needed a facility purpose-built for internet streaming delivery, with different requirements around encoding, transcoding, and global distribution than traditional broadcast operations.

### Strategic Vision
Eric identified that AOL's existing Broadcast Operations Center could not meet projected growth in streaming; secured multi-million-dollar budget and executive approval to design, build, and lead a new all-digital facility purpose-built for OTT and streaming content.

### Technical Approach
- **All-Digital Architecture:** First facility purpose-designed for digital streaming rather than broadcast/satellite
- **Redundancy & Reliability:** Built for 24/7 operations supporting continuous video/audio delivery
- **Multi-Channel Capacity:** Designed to handle 100+ radio channels and 34+ video channels simultaneously
- **Production Integration:** Included dedicated multi-camera production studio for live event production
- **Scalability:** Architecture designed to scale as streaming content volume grew
- **Vendor Ecosystem:** Managed complete vendor lifecycle for equipment and services

### Facility Specifications
- **Radio Channels:** 100+ channels
- **Video Channels:** 34+ channels
- **Production:** Dedicated multi-camera production studio
- **24x7 Operations:** Fully staffed for continuous content delivery
- **Location:** Sterling, VA

### Operational Capabilities
- **Live Event Production:** Hosted live event production (Live 8, news events, etc.)
- **Continuous Encoding:** Real-time transcoding and encoding for streaming delivery
- **Multi-Format Output:** Generated multiple bitrate/codec variants for streaming platforms
- **Global Distribution:** Content distribution to worldwide streaming audience
- **News & Linear:** 24x7 news feed production and linear channel programming

### Major Achievements
- **Industry First:** First all-digital broadcast operations center purpose-designed for streaming/OTT
- **Live 8 Success:** Successfully supported production of Emmy Award-winning Live 8 global concert event
- **Scale:** Managed 100+ radio and 34+ video channels simultaneously
- **Reliability:** Maintained 24x7 operations supporting continuous streaming
- **Vendor Management:** Full lifecycle management of complex vendor ecosystem
- **Executive Buy-In:** Secured multi-million-dollar budget and executive approval for facility design and build

### Technologies & Tools
- **Video Encoding:** Real-time encoding and transcoding for streaming delivery
- **Studio Production:** Multi-camera production equipment and switching
- **Streaming Infrastructure:** Early 2000s streaming technology (RealVideo, Windows Media, etc.)
- **Content Management:** Broadcast operations management systems
- **Distribution:** Early CDN technology for global content delivery

- **Architecture Model:** Influenced industry thinking about streaming operations centers

- **Audience:** Millions of viewers accessing AOL streaming content
