# TWCTV Application
## 2012 – 2017
## Time Warner Cable

### Summary
Emmy Award-winning television application delivering video on demand and live television to Time Warner Cable subscribers. Key architect for VOD and advertising subsystems, implementing DRM security, dynamic ad insertion, and centralized content delivery. Transitioned Time Warner Cable from legacy set-top box VOD architecture to next-generation application supporting both traditional and OTT delivery.

### Business Context
Time Warner Cable needed to modernize its video-on-demand and streaming capabilities to compete with emerging OTT platforms while maintaining security and advertising revenue generation. TWCTV application provided unified interface for TVE (TV Everywhere) access across set-top boxes and web/mobile applications, requiring sophisticated DRM and dynamic ad insertion.

### Technical Approach
- **Unified Architecture:** Single application supporting set-top box, web, and mobile platforms
- **DRM Implementation:** Just-in-time encryption providing robust content protection
- **Dynamic Ad Insertion:** Server-side ad insertion (SSAI) for targeted monetization
- **VOD Distribution:** Centralized VOD content acquisition and distribution pipeline
- **Scalability:** Architecture supporting millions of TWC subscribers
- **Standards Compliance:** Broadcast-quality delivery with regulatory compliance

### Architectural Challenges
- **OTT Adoption Recognition:** Anticipated rapid growth in OTT consumption and architected accordingly
- **Prototype Development:** Built prototypes that informed TWCTV Application architecture decisions
- **Platform Consolidation:** Unified VOD delivery across set-top box and OTT platforms
- **Vendor Coordination:** Managed multiple hardware, software, and SaaS providers simultaneously
