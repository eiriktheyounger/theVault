# Olympic Daily Recap — AI-Powered Personalized Highlights

## Project Summary

AI-driven personalized highlight playlist system for Paris 2024 Olympics featuring synthesized Al Michaels narration. Architected content workflows generating 7M+ personalized video variants. Demonstrated massive-scale personalization using generative AI at production scale during record-breaking streaming event.

## Role & Contribution

**Sole Architect for Content Workflows**: Designed and operated the end-to-end acquisition pipeline for highlight clips and metadata. Did not build underlying AI voice synthesis platform—architected the full content workflows that fed it.

## Key Responsibilities

- Led ingest and processing for both editorial and FFMPEG-based clip generation
- Integrated RAG-compatible metadata enrichment from internal and public sources
- Supported legal and compliance reviews, especially for AI-generated voice and Olympic footage
- Scoped and onboarded 3rd-party AI voice vendor for Al Michaels clone using licensed NBC Sports archival material
- Selected iOS/tvOS for playback platforms due to reach and stability
- Collaborated with VP lead architect (playlist generation) and acted as primary contact for marketing, Olympics, and security teams

## Key Results

- **Architecture supported daily personalized playlists for millions of users**
- **Generated 7M+ personalized video variants** with synthesized Al Michaels narration (SAG-AFTRA compliant)
- NBCU processed over 5,000 hours of footage using AI
- Platform proved stable despite mid-Olympics voiceover challenges
- Work was politically high-visibility and showcased NBCU's AI capabilities globally
- Featured in Emmy-winning interactive experience submission

## Scale & Impact

- 7M+ unique personalized variations generated
- Al Michaels synthesized voice across all variants (SAG-AFTRA compliant)
- Millions of viewers received personalized recap recommendations
- Contributed to:
  - 23.5 billion streaming minutes
  - 82% viewership increase vs. Tokyo 2020
  - All-time streaming record for Peacock platform

## Technology Stack

- FFMPEG clip generation from source footage
- RAG-compatible metadata enrichment
- AI voice synthesis platform (3rd party)
- Personalization pipeline for variant generation
- iOS/tvOS playback optimization
- Content compliance and legal review systems
