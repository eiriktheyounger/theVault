# Patent US-10375452 — Apparatus and Methods for Thumbnail Generation

**Status:** Granted (2019)  
**Assignee:** NBCUniversal  
**Category:** Video Processing

## Overview

Apparatus and methods for automated thumbnail generation from video content. Patent granted during early NBCUniversal tenure, representing continued evolution of video metadata automation expertise.

## Technical Innovation

### Thumbnail Generation Technology
- Automated frame analysis and scoring
- Composition-based key frame selection
- Intelligent thumbnail extraction from video streams
- Multi-variant generation for different contexts

### Applications
- Streaming platform content discovery
- TV guide artwork and metadata
- Interactive UI thumbnail display
- Content recommendation interfaces

## Career Context

**Timeframe:** Invented during early NBCUniversal era (2019)  
**Role Context:** Initial years at NBCUniversal before major interactive and AI/ML platform work

### Strategic Importance
While this patent predates Live Actions and Daily Recap work, it represents the metadata automation foundation that enabled later interactive personalization features.
