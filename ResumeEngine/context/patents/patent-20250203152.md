# Patent: Timed Metadata for Overlays
## Patent Number: 20250203152
## Status: Pending
## Assignee: NBCUniversal

### Summary
Apparatus and methods for applying timed metadata to create synchronized overlays on live and VOD video content. Enables precise timing of graphical elements and interactive components aligned with broadcast action and metadata events. Patent covers fundamental technology underlying Live Actions product and other interactive sports features.

### Technical Scope
- Timed metadata generation and management
- Overlay synchronization with video content
- VOD and live delivery support
- Real-time timing control and coordination

### Applications
- **Live Actions Feature:** Time-synced overlays for live sports events
- **Interactive Sports:** Real-time graphical elements aligned with game action
- **VOD Enhancement:** Post-broadcast addition of overlays to on-demand content
- **Olympics Coverage:** Interactive elements during Olympic event broadcasts

### Related Projects
- Live Actions (2021-2025)
- Interactive content platform for live and VOD programming

### Business Impact
- Foundation for interactive sports products
- Differentiated viewer experience vs. traditional broadcast
- Key competitive advantage for NBCU streaming platforms
