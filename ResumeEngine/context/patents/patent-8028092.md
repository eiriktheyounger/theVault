# Patent US-8028092 — Inserting Advertising Content

**Status:** Granted (2011)  
**Assignee:** AOL  
**Category:** Advertising Technology

## Overview

Methods for inserting advertising content into media streams. First advertising patent, granted in 2011, protects core ad insertion innovation from AOL era (2001-2006).

## Technical Innovation

### Ad Insertion Technology
- Stream-based advertising insertion
- Content splicing and ad replacement
- Dynamic advertisement delivery
- Monetization through content stream manipulation

### Applications
- Live streaming advertising
- Video on-demand ad insertion
- Real-time content stream manipulation
- Broadcast advertising systems

## Business Impact

**Era:** Pioneering live streaming and ad insertion (pre-YouTube, early broadband)  
**Innovation:** Advanced ad insertion techniques during emerging streaming era

## Career Context

**Timeframe:** Invented during AOL tenure (2001-2006)  
**Role Context:** Early career at AOL during pioneering streaming era

### Strategic Importance
- First patent in ad insertion series (granted 2011)
- Pioneering innovation in streaming advertising
- Foundation for later ad tech work at TWC and NBCUniversal

## Patent Portfolio Context

First in **4-patent advertising technology series**:
- **Granted:** US-8028092 (AOL, Dynamic insertion of advertising content into media streams) ← **this patent**; US-8762575 (Facebook, Enhanced targeting techniques within streaming video); US-8769151 (Facebook, Synchronizing content insertion with media playback)
- **Pending:** US-20240430496 (NBCU, Dynamic In-Scene Secondary Content Insertion)

### Career Progression
- **2011:** US-8028092 granted (AOL era ad insertion)
