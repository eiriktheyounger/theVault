# Patent: Dynamic In-Scene Secondary Content Insertion
## Patent Number: 20240430496
## Status: Pending
## Assignee: NBCUniversal

### Summary
Apparatus and methods for dynamically inserting secondary content (graphics, metadata, advertisements) into video scenes in real-time while maintaining visual coherence and technical quality. Enables insertion of personalized or contextual elements without interrupting primary video content. Patent covers technology supporting both traditional ad insertion and interactive content features.

### Technical Scope
- In-scene content insertion and positioning
- Real-time dynamic insertion capabilities
- Visual coherence and quality maintenance
- Personalization and context-based insertion
- Broadcast-quality delivery standards

### Applications
- **Dynamic Ad Insertion:** Targeted advertisements seamlessly integrated into scenes
- **Interactive Content:** Supplementary information or interactive elements without content interruption
- **Sports Overlays:** Stats, player information, and interactive elements overlaid on game footage
- **Personalization:** Individual viewer-specific content inserted dynamically
- **Live & VOD:** Support for both live event and on-demand delivery
