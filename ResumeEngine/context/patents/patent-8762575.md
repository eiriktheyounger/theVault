**Assignee:** Facebook
**Title (descriptive):** Enhanced targeting techniques within streaming video
**Category:** Advertising Technology

## Overview

Methods for enhanced targeting within streaming video. Granted to Facebook in 2014.

### Pattern of Innovation

- Evolution: insertion → targeting → synchronization → dynamic in-scene integration
