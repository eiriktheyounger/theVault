# Patent US-8762575 — Inserting Advertising Content

**Status:** Granted (2014)  
**Assignee:** Facebook  
**Category:** Advertising Technology

## Overview

Methods for inserting advertising content into video streams. Patent represents core advertising insertion technology at Time Warner Cable during the OTT transition.

## Technical Innovation

### Ad Insertion Technology
- Real-time ad insertion methods
- Content stream manipulation and workflow
- Dynamic ad decision-making
- Integrated advertising platforms

### Applications
- VOD advertising delivery
- Linear and near-linear content ad insertion
- Dynamic replacement advertising
- Monetization platform core

## Business Impact

**Revenue Contribution:** Part of ad technology strategy driving 350%+ ad revenue increase  
**Scale:** Deployed across TWC cable footprint

## Career Context

**Timeframe:** Invented during Time Warner Cable tenure (2006-2017)  
**Role Context:** Sr. Solutions Architect, VOD Systems and OTT transition
