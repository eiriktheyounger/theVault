# Patent US-11310567 — Apparatus and Methods for Thumbnail Generation

**Status:** Granted (2022)  
**Assignee:** Time Warner Cable  
**Category:** Video Processing

## Overview

Apparatus and methods for automated thumbnail generation from video content. Patent demonstrates video processing innovation during Time Warner Cable VOD and OTT transition era.

## Technical Innovation

### Thumbnail Generation Technology
- Frame analysis and selection algorithms
- Metadata-driven thumbnail logic
- Multi-resolution thumbnail generation
- Automated artwork extraction

### Applications
- VOD catalog interfaces
- OTT platform content discovery
- TV guide and EPG thumbnails
- Content recommendation systems

## Career Context

**Timeframe:** Invented during Time Warner Cable era (2006-2017)  
**Role Context:** Principal Engineer, VOD Systems (2006-2012)

### Business Impact
Thumbnail automation contributed to efficient VOD catalog management supporting millions of cable customers accessing on-demand content.
