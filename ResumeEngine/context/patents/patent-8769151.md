# Patent US-8769151 — Adding Advertising Content to Media Content

**Status:** Granted (2014)  
**Assignee:** Time Warner Cable  
**Category:** Advertising Technology

## Overview

Methods for adding advertising content to media content. Patent protects core dynamic ad insertion technology deployed across Time Warner Cable's video delivery infrastructure.

## Technical Innovation

### Ad Insertion Technology
- Dynamic content insertion into media streams
- Ad delivery and workflow orchestration
- Monetization platform innovation
- Real-time ad decision-making

### Applications
- Cable VOD advertising
- Linear video ad insertion
- SSAI (Server-Side Ad Insertion)
- CSAI (Client-Side Ad Insertion)

## Business Impact

**Revenue Generation:** Part of ad insertion strategy contributing to 350%+ ad revenue increase  
**Scale:** Deployed across millions of TWC cable subscribers

## Career Context

**Timeframe:** Invented during Time Warner Cable era (2006-2017)  
**Role Context:** Sr. Solutions Architect, VOD Systems (2006-2012)

### Strategic Importance
- Core monetization technology for cable operator
- Foundation for TWC's competitive advertising capabilities
- Enabled transition from linear to dynamic ad insertion
