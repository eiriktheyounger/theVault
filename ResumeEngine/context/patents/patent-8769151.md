# Patent US-8769151 — Adding Advertising Content to Media Content

**Status:** Granted (2014)
**Assignee:** Facebook
**Title (descriptive):** Synchronizing content insertion with media playback
**Category:** Advertising Technology

## Overview

Methods for synchronizing the insertion of advertising and secondary content with media playback. Granted to Facebook in 2014.

## Technical Innovation

### Ad Insertion Technology
- Synchronized ad/content insertion with playback timing
- Dynamic content insertion into media streams
- Ad delivery and workflow orchestration
- Real-time ad decision-making

### Applications
- VOD advertising
- Linear video ad insertion
- SSAI (Server-Side Ad Insertion)
- CSAI (Client-Side Ad Insertion)

## Patent Portfolio Context

Part of **4-patent advertising technology series**:
- **Granted:** US-8028092 (AOL, Dynamic insertion of advertising content into media streams); US-8762575 (Facebook, Enhanced targeting techniques within streaming video); US-8769151 (Facebook, Synchronizing content insertion with media playback) ← **this patent**
- **Pending:** US-20240430496 (NBCU, Dynamic In-Scene Secondary Content Insertion)

### Advertising Technology Leadership
- Two Facebook ad-tech patents granted same year (targeting + synchronization)
- Multiple assignees across the series (AOL, Facebook, NBCU) demonstrate ad-tech depth
- Evolution: insertion → targeting → synchronization → dynamic in-scene integration
