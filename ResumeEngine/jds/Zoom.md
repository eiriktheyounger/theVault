## About the job

**What You Can Expect** Reporting directly to the Chief Digital Information Officer you will drive enterprise-wide AI adoption that delivers measurable business impact. Partnering with executive leadership, you'll define and execute AI strategy at scale. You'll solve complex challenges using cutting-edge technologies while creating lasting organizational value.  
  
**About The Team** We deliver digital transformation initiatives that reshape how our organization operates. Our team partners across business units to integrate emerging technologies into core processes. We exist to accelerate innovation and drive competitive advantage through AI.  
  
**Responsibilities**

- Developing and executing AI transformation roadmaps aligned with business objectives and digital strategy
- Identifying and prioritizing opportunities for operational efficiency, revenue growth, and customer experience enhancement through AI solutions
- Leading cross-functional initiatives that integrate AI capabilities into core business processes with measurable outcomes
- Building relationships with business unit leaders to understand pain points and translate them into AI use cases
- Communicating AI strategy, progress, and business impact to executive leadership and board members

**What We're Looking For**

- Demonstrate 10+ years of business transformation experience leveraging technology to drive organizational change
- Lead enterprise-wide AI transformation initiatives with proven, measurable business outcomes
- Influence C-level executives and shape strategic decisions through effective stakeholder management
- Apply working knowledge of AI/ML technologies including generative AI, NLP, and predictive analytics
- Translate technical concepts into business value using analytical and strategic thinking skills
- Build business cases, ROI models, and value realization frameworks that drive investment decisions
- Hold bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, or demonstrate equivalent practical experience
- Contribute published thought leadership or speaking experience at industry conferences
