## By Topic

### AI/ML Innovation
- **How to frame:** Applied AI/LLM orchestration to real business problems
- **Examples:**
  - Key Plays feature (AI/ML near real-time sports highlights)
  - Olympic Daily Recap (GenAI with celebrity voice)
  - RE Kreations LLM pipeline (multi-model orchestration)
- **Never:** Overpromise; stick to what's documented
- **Avoid:** Generic "AI expertise" — always provide specific technologies and outcomes

### Ad Tech & Monetization
- **How to frame:** Technical architect of monetization systems that increased revenue
- **Examples:**
  - SSAI/CSAI implementations (200%+ revenue growth)
  - Dynamic ad insertion (DAI) for VOD
  - Ad tech product innovation (Key Plays, interactive ads)
- **Emphasize:** Business impact + technical complexity
- **Avoid:** "Advertising expert" — position as technical solution architect

### Leadership & Scale
- **When to claim:** Only when you designed/architected something at scale
- **Examples:**
  - "Served millions of users" ✓
  - "Operated multi-CDN delivery" ✓
  - "Led teams of X" ✗ (avoid unless critical to role)
- **Better framing:** "Architected systems handling [scale]" vs. "Led team of Y"
- **Avoid:** Headcount-focused leadership language

### Industry Firsts
- **AOL BOC:** "First all-digital broadcast operations center for streaming/OTT" ✓
- **TWCTV:** "Early prototypes that informed TWCTV Application architecture" ✓
- **Live 8:** "World record concurrent streaming (Live 8 Concert)" ✓
- **Never:** Claim firsts without documentation
- **Tone:** Matter-of-fact, specific, historically anchored

### Remote Work & Distributed Teams
- **Emphasize:** 11+ years remote experience (2014–Present)
- **Frame as strength:** Self-directed, asynchronous communication, vendor management across timezones
- **Use:** When relevant to role expectations
- **Avoid:** Apologetic tone; remote work is an asset here

### Awards & Patents
- **Emmys:** Prominent, always mention; pair with specific achievement
- **Patents:** Include only on resumes requesting technical depth; provide context
- **Certifications:** AWS Media & Entertainment relevant; include if space permits
- **Rule:** Never oversell; let credentials speak

## Sensitive Topics

### Organizational Departures
**Rule: "Organizational restructuring" ONLY**
- One sentence: "During organizational restructuring in [DATE], I transitioned..."
- Then: Pivot immediately to accomplishments
- Never elaborate on reasons, timing, or personal impact
- Use as opportunity to showcase resilience/landing on feet

### Employment Gaps or Short Tenures
- **Leidos (1 year):** Frame as "focused engagement" or "strategic consulting"
- **Ooyala (1 year):** Position as "SaaS solutions architecture specialization"
- **Focus:** Quality of work and learning, not duration
- **Pattern:** Acknowledge implicitly through achievements, don't explain

### Technical Depth vs. Breadth
- **When applying to deep IC roles:** Emphasize breadth of architecture (full stack)
- **When applying to specialist roles:** Emphasize depth in specific domain (ad tech, streaming, AI)
- **Pattern:** Let the job description guide which accomplishments you elevate

### Startup/Founder Work (RE Kreations)
- **For corporate roles:** "Founder-led platform" (sounds serious)
- **For startup roles:** "Full product lifecycle from MVP to production"
- **For VC roles:** "Autonomous R&D and product development"
- **Avoid:** "Side project" or "hobby"; position as serious entrepreneurial work
