# Banned Words — Never Use in Resume Output

## Absolute Bans
- spearheaded
- leveraged
- revolutionized
- best-in-class
- thought leader
- passionate about
- results-driven
- dynamic professional
- synergy
- impactful

## Avoid Unless Explicitly Accurate
- helped (use "built" if he built it)
- assisted
- collaborated on (too vague — specify the actual contribution)
- led the team (be specific about what changed as a result)

## Framing Rules

### NBCUniversal Departure (2021–2025)
- **Mandatory framing:** "organizational restructuring" ONLY
- **Length:** Two sentences maximum, then redirect to accomplishments
- **Never mention:** Personal circumstances, specific individuals, timing details
- **Pattern:** "During organizational restructuring in [DATE], I transitioned from NBCU. Accomplishments during tenure included..."

### Company Descriptions
- **Harmonic:** Cloud-native broadcast and OTT solutions leader
- **Ooyala:** Media lifecycle SaaS provider
- **Time Warner Cable:** Cable/OTT video and streaming pioneer
- **AOL:** Early streaming and digital media pioneer
- **Leidos:** Government IT solutions provider
- **RE Kreations LLC:** Founder-led AI/automation orchestration platform

## Resume Structure Rules
- No objective statement
- No "references available upon request"
- Accomplishments first, responsibilities second
- Bullet points start with a verb, end with outcome/metric
- Technical details should demonstrate business impact
- Avoid vague phrases; be specific about what changed

## Voice Requirements
- Direct, technical, outcome-focused
- Emphasize: innovation, scale, architecture decisions, business results
- Avoid: corporate jargon, passive voice, self-promotion language
- Pattern: "Designed X that resulted in Y" (action + impact)
