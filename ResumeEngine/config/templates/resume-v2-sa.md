## Notes for Generation
- **Do not use banned words:** spearheaded, leveraged, revolutionized, best-in-class, thought leader, passionate about, results-driven, dynamic professional, synergy, impactful
- **Customer/business first:** Every bullet should lead with customer outcome, customer name (if allowed), RFP win, or revenue impact
- **Platform expertise:** If the JD mentions specific platforms (e.g., AWS, Kubernetes, Salesforce), emphasize relevant architecture experience
- **RFP wins & POCs:** Quantify customer wins: "3 Fortune 500 accounts", "$8M ARR", "5 tier-1 broadcasters"
- **Strategic positioning:** Use "architect", "advisor", "strategist" over "engineer" or "developer"
- **Remote work:** Mention 11+ years remote + distributed team success if relevant
- **Enterprise focus:** Emphasize C-suite alignment, long-term partnerships, vendor evaluation experience
- **Quantify impact:** Revenue lift %, cost savings, reliability improvements (99.99% uptime), time-to-market reduction
